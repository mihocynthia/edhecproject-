Outreach Programs

What we do?
When it comes to defining how the homeless live, what their needs are, many negative feelings and stereotypes overwhelm our thoughts. But we should first ask ourselves: do we really know what they experience daily? Our approach was to directly speak to them to have a better picture of their living conditions. After listening to them, we could think of how to best meet their needs, in the simplest and most efficient way. Our initiative relies on a simple basis: empowering the people that will help the homeless empower themselves.

What we are looking for?
1) People who are engaged and committed to make the world a better place for all
2) Structures, stores and companies that will support our initiative.
3) Our initiative to be advertised by committed people who believe in our project.

Volunteer Opportunities

How to Help?

3 Easy ways to get involved:
A) Deliver goods directly to homeless people
B) Join us to craft the “Partnerships Outreach” and help the ones in need gain a wider variety of resources from stores, companies, pharmacies...
C) Become an Ambassador for 1-to-1 and do the Outreach Programs with us at other community centers and churches, to raise awareness about our association so we can continue to help those in need.
