Outreach Programs

How to help?
During our outreach and research, 1 to 1 realize that nobody wants to start being homeless. Stereotype that homeless people, only do drugs and are dirty and shouldn’t be help. Reality hits everyone has their point in time, where we all hit a low point, and having a helping hand a source of hope. Our association is searching for ways to help and assist homeless. We see homeless as one to one, as one self since we are all humans let’s try to assist.
