Offer Kindness

Our goal is to ensure the homeless have he necessities that they need. Before starting 1 to 1 we did conducted a through research. We found out that homeless, wanted soap, food, coffee and clothing the most. We have hopes that we can provide these necessities for them, so they can improve their lifestyle. There are websites and programs out there that already have shelters and clothing. The strengths of our organization focuses on the daily necessities to improve their daily comfort.

Their Stories

Homeless

Michael, Shattuck Avenue
Originally From Chicago, Illinois, he left his family and crossed the United States by bus to find a job. More than 2,000 miles away from his homeland, he used to work in restaurants, cleaning dishes. Sometimes he would work as a waiter. But after having four or five jobs, he decided to quit. He ended up living on the street. Three months have passed. He's still left with no money or job.
