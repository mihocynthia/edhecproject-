Partners & Sponsors

Our Partners

We are happy to announce we have a sponsor. Every vision, needs to start somewhere, and to be able to assist homeless, we also need businesses support. We are grateful to Way Brew Station, Berkeley Food & Housing Project, 42 Silicon Valley School, and Edhec Business School.

After research, we realized homeless, also enjoys coffee, especially in Berkeley. Coffee is as popular for homeless, as for people with homes, and occupations; we decided to approach coffee shops to see how we can bring more coffee to the homeless and we did it! We successfully got a coffee shop to help our association give out coffee to homeless.

See how your business can contribute and bring more smiles to the homeless by emailing us.
