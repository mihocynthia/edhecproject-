Outreach in Berkeley

Outreach at Berkeley's Food And Housing Project

Outreach in Berkeley, Jan 16, 2020

Recently, our founder Pauline took the initiative to contact Berkeley Food and Housing Project, a local association which aims at helping homeless people locally. She promoted our project and received insightful advice. She also volunteered for a community meal offering at the Lutheran Church of the Cross. We are thankful for the Berkeley Food & Housing Project association for giving us the opportunity and information we need to make this project happen. This project wouldn't have been done without the help of our community.
