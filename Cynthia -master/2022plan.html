2022 Plan


Given the situation the world is currently undergoing with the COVID-19, we have adjusted our program which was initially planned to start in 2020. We are proud to launch our Outreach initiative, the 1-to-1 project for early 2021, giving time for the economy to recover and for people to donate and demonstrate solidarity among their communities. Our objectives do not change though, we expect as many volunteers and donations as possible in the years to come! Creating a positive impact, empowering the ones in need, giving homeless people access to goods they are usually deprived of, is our goal, your goal! Live on a one-to-one standard.
